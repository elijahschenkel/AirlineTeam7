About Iowa Airline

Iowa Airlines is an up and coming regional airline with its headquarter and major hub at Eastern Iowa Airport in Cedar Rapids, IA. The airline operates over 250 flights daily and serves 5 continental US destinations.

Here at Iowa Airline, we pride ourselves on quality service from our landing page to landing at your final destination.

Fly comfortably. While our competitors are finding ways to add more seats to already cramped plans, Iowa Air decided not to follow that trend and instead expand our fleet.

Who says good customer service only has to be in the air, at Iowa Air we understand that flying can be stressful. Unwind and relax in our private members room, where we offer complimentary water, coffee, tea, and soda. And don't worry about those pesky reward cards, simply scan your ticket at the door and you'll be granted access.
